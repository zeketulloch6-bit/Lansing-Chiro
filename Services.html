What a first visit looks like

  1. Intake + history: what’s happening and what you want to change
  2. Assessment: range of motion, posture, movement checks
  3. Plan: recommended frequency + home routine
  4. Care: if appropriate, treatment begins the same day

We’ll refer out if your symptoms suggest you need a different type of care.
